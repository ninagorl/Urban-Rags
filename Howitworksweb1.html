How it
Works
Donating
made easy...

We encourage our community to utilize
our drop boxes around Houston to donate
any and all clothes. Just drop off and dip
and wait for the fashion magic to happen!
Click the donate tab in the menu bar to
see all of our donation box locations!

Making the
magic...

As the great saying goes-reduce, reuse,
recycle!Our susty crew expresses their
creativity through upcycling any and all
donated clothing items. Being able to
repurpose fabric and give it an exciting
new life is where the magic begins.

Look good,
feel good,
do good...

Look good and feel good while doing good
for the planet! We love a great fit, and we
love the confidence that it gives you. We
are on a mission to help you build a more
thoughtful wardrobe, while educating our
community on sustainability in fashion.
