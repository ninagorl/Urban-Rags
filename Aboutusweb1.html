About Us
Who we are...

We are a local non-profit based out of our beloved Houston, Texas who is changing the status quo of how we can encourage our
community to donate, repurpose, and upcycle clothing fabrics. Our sustainable (susty) efforts to changing how we repurpose
and upcycle fabrics impact how we can slow down fast fashion and create a more positive impact on our environment

Our Values...

We work to center these values in all that we do.
We aim to inspire, educate, and motivate action
towards change in a way that is approachable,
accessible, and encouraging! Inclusivity,
transparency, sustainability, community, and
fairness is what we strive to accomplish!
